

## How to compose a table in markdown file?
For example, the following talbe can be done in at least two ways:
|            | **1209 Hz** | **1336 Hz** | **1477 Hz** |
|------------|:-----------:|:-----------:|:-----------:|
| **697 Hz** |      1      |      2      |      3      |
| **770 Hz** |      4      |      5      |      6      |
| **852 Hz** |      7      |      8      |      9      |
| **941 Hz** |      *      |      0      |      #      |

- **1st way**
```markdown
|            | **1209 Hz** | **1336 Hz** | **1477 Hz** |
|------------|-------------|-------------|-------------|
| **697 Hz** |      1      |      2      |      3      |
| **770 Hz** |      4      |      5      |      6      |
| **852 Hz** |      7      |      8      |      9      |
| **941 Hz** |      *      |      0      |      #      |
```
- **2nd way**
```markdown
|            | **1209 Hz** | **1336 Hz** | **1477 Hz** |
|------------|:-----------:|:-----------:|:-----------:|
| **697 Hz** |      1      |      2      |      3      |
| **770 Hz** |      4      |      5      |      6      |
| **852 Hz** |      7      |      8      |      9      |
| **941 Hz** |      *      |      0      |      #      |
```
